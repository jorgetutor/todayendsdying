Today ends dying

About

Today Ends Dying borns without any musical label, with the only purpose to find their own creative space within the musical scene nowadays. The band began in the summer of 2004. In the Summer of 2005 the band begins to record their first Demo Sometimes it seems to be yours in Gruposmadrid.com studios.

Members
